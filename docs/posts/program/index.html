Program

The 38th Annual Conference on Human Sentence Processing will be held March 27-29, 2025 at the University of Maryland. HSP 2025 will be an in-person conference.

Invited Speakers

We are delighted to welcome the following six invited speakers (in alphabetical order)

  • Dr. Andrew Begel, Carnegie Mellon University
  • Dr. Rain Bosworth, Rochester Institute of Technology
  • Dr. Stefanie Kuchinsky, Walter Reed National Military Medical Center
  • Dr. Philip Resnik, University of Maryland, College Park
  • Dr. Charlotte Vaughn, University of Maryland, College Park
